What Is Jujutsu Kaisen?

The Jujutsu Kaisen manga series is written and illustrated by Gege Akutami, while the Jujutsu Kaisen anime series is produced by MAPPA. Both manga and anime have influenced the real world, with fans pursuing all kinds of cosplay, body art, and more.

Jujutsu Kaisen centers on a high school student named Yuji Itadori, who swallows a rotting finger that belonged to a powerful Curse and subsequently becomes possessed. Jujutsu Sorcerers sentence him to death by exorcism, but before that happens, he’s tasked with finding and consuming the Curse’s remaining fingers.

The main premise of the series may sound like another boy copycat anime series that revives the same metaphors as Naruto, One Piece, and Bleach, but when you look at the first episode, it's categorized into genres and unique. You can see that it gives a sense.

But why is Jujutsu Kaisen so popular?

To get started, the series features comedies with action-adventure, drama touches, and mystery dashes that really inspire plots. Like the other Boys series, Jujutsu Kaisen features many tense battles and action-packed rivals.

Ryomen Sukuna is the most fearsome Curse and strongest one of his time—and it’s his finger that Yuji swallowed.

The Jujutsu Sorcerers are tasked with exorcising Curses of varying levels, from low-level monsters to special-grade monstrocities.

The Unique Tale of Jujutsu Kaisen

The protagonist is part of theJujutsu Tech School, an organization that specializes in cursing demons. There they learn how to handle different types of demon slaughter to save humanity from the horrifying curses lurking in the streets and communities.
